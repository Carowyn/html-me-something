Work Flow

How I got where I am going.

❖ Where I began.

         

I started off working in high school at Burger King. I left that job after a couple months because my grades dropped a little, and they wouldn't let me reduce
hours or get off earlier. A couple years later, as a senior in high school, I worked at Walmart. I gained my basis in customer service there.

I took Keyboarding each year in highschool, until as a Senior I took a technical class specifically in Microsoft Powerpoint. I've always been interested in computers,
I just didn't know what path to take.

As an adult, my work history has been rocky while trying to find the right fit between what I can do, and what I am good at. Between 2003 and 2009, due to being
in a manipulative relationship that affected working, I prefer not to discuss much about the lenghth of jobs, and focus instead on skills I learned.

Most of my positions have involved customer service. However, that is not where my interests lie. I prefer things a bit less...peopley. I don't want to answer phone
call after phone call to just be yelled at. I don't want to stand at a counter and take an order. I don't want to scan everything in and cash people out.What I can
bring from those positions, though, is an ability to handle client requests, the ability to listen to what the client wants and translate it into what I can do or
make for them. I also have the ability to learn whatever job and position specific skills are needed.

In May of 2009, I went to Job Corps. I was tired of not being able to stay in any certain job for any real length of time. I thought that maybe getting away and learning
some "real" skills would help with that. Because I have my high school diploma and because I tested well, I was able to just start with the Advanced Training program there. This
one was TCU - Transportation Communication Union. I learned math and clerical skills, as well as additional Keyboarding. I assisted other students with learning Microsoft
Office among other things. I also completed training for Rail Freight Clerk. When I completed training a year later. I had finally discovered the toxicity of the
relationship I was in, and I chose to stay in the Kansas City area instead of going home.

I've tried several different things since that time, and also fell back on my customer service skills as well. None of it has been what I enjoy.

❖ Where I'm at.

Well, technically right now I'm sitting in my living room, a few days away from starting the second section of a LaunchCode class. ☺

Right now, I've learned Python basics, and am in the process of learning HTML and CSS. I anticipate learning JavaScript in this unit as well. Afterwards, they
will give us a choice between 3 different area specific tracks that will lead us towards having job ready skills in our geographical area. I'm not currently working.
I wanted to give this program my entire focus so that I can learn everything fully. I LOVE this class so far. It hasbeen so challenging, and I get so veryexcited when a new thing works!

I am(was?) part of Phi Theta Kappa while working on a 2 year A.S. in Biology. Due to life, I had to stop to try to work some more. While I love Biology, I think that much
extra schooling at this time in my life won't put me on the path to a career until too late to grow adequately in it. I am finding that I love learning this even more than I
love Biology!!!

❖ Where I'm going.

         

My goal is to land an internship through LaunchCode. From there, I want to learn as much as I can, as fully as I can so I can excel at the position I'm interning for.
I want this to be a career I can learn and grow in until retirement. This is a life- changing opportunity for me!
